My Bio

My name is Sarah Somers and I live in rural Nevada. I have been a school teacher for the past 8 years and prior to that had positions in district administrative duties and banking. I have started a journey in web programming and web design and have enjoyed learning something new! I chose this path because I wanted to do something new and it intrigued me. From that point, I realized I could enjoy it because I enjoy complex problem solving, design, and even love math, all of which seem to be helpful in the world of programming and web design. Aside from work, I love travel, running and fitness, my dogs, reading and spending time with my teenage kids. I also own a business, a fitness gym, which has been such a fun challenge. I look forward to starting my new chapter in web design and programming. Please reach out to me with any questions!
